OUR FILOSOPHY

Located in the heart of Bloorcourt Village, Filosophy serves delicious freshly baked pies and pastries, delectable ice cream treats and a great cup of coffee!

This cozy nook specializes in traditional Greek-inspired filo pastry pies, savoury meat and vegan pies and classic baked goods. All products are baked fresh daily and are locally sourced.

Whether you are looking for a quick breakfast bite, a hearty lunch or scrumptious dessert, Filosophy has something for everyone in a warm and inviting atmosphere.
